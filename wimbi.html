prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : vegetable lunch vegetable soup food

instructions

step 1

                
                Sauté Vegetables:

                In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions and garlic, sautéing until they become translucent.
                Add Vegetables:
                
                Add carrots, celery, bell pepper, zucchini, and green beans to the pot. Cook for about 5-7 minutes until the vegetables start to soften.
                Add Tomatoes:
                
                Pour in the diced tomatoes (with their juices) and stir to combine with the vegetables.
                Pour in Broth:
                
                Add vegetable broth to the pot. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to simmer.
                Season:
                
                Add thyme, oregano, salt, and pepper to the soup. Adjust the seasoning according to your taste.
                Optional Pasta:
                
                If you want to add pasta to your soup, do so at this point. Cook the soup until the pasta is al dente or according to the package instructions.
                Simmer:
                
                Allow the soup to simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until all the vegetables are tender.
                Adjust Seasoning:
                
                Taste the soup and adjust the seasoning if necessary. Add more salt or pepper as needed.

step 2

Serve: Ladle the soup into bowls, and garnish with fresh parsley.

step 3

Enjoy: Serve the vegetable soup hot and enjoy with crusty bread or your favorite crackers.

ingredients

1 tablespoon olive oil 1 onion, chopped 2 cloves garlic, minced 3 carrots, peeled and sliced 2 celery stalks, sliced 1 bell pepper, chopped (any color) 1 zucchini, diced 1 cup green beans, chopped 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes 6 cups vegetable broth 1 teaspoon dried thyme 1 teaspoon dried oregano Salt and pepper to taste 1 cup pasta (optional) Fresh parsley for garnish
