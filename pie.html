instructions

step 1

                Prepare the Marinade:

In a mixing bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, red wine vinegar, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried thyme, paprika, ground cumin, salt, black pepper, and optional crushed red pepper flakes.
Prepare the Ribs:

If necessary, remove the membrane from the back of the ribs.
Season the ribs with salt and black pepper.
Marinate the Ribs:

Place the ribs in a large dish or a resealable plastic bag.
Pour the marinade over the ribs, ensuring they are well coated.
Marinate in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight.
Preheat the Grill or Oven:

Preheat your grill to medium-high heat or preheat your oven to 375째F (190째C).
Grill or Roast the Ribs:

Grill the ribs for about 20-30 minutes per side, or roast them in the oven for approximately 1.5 to 2 hours, or until the internal temperature reaches 145째F (63째C) for pork.
Baste the ribs with the marinade or your favorite barbecue sauce during the last 15-20 minutes of cooking.

step 2

                Rest and Serve:

Allow the ribs to rest for a few minutes before slicing.
Garnish with chopped fresh parsley and serve with lemon wedges.
